## Problem Statement

The strategy currently treats an opposing EMA(8/21) cross as a **momentum failure signal**, but not as an explicit exit condition.  
While this avoids overreacting to noise, it also creates an ambiguity:

- EMA crosses are **lagging indicators**, especially on 1‑minute charts.
- Momentum can fail structurally (rotation, absorption, distribution) **before** EMAs cross.
- Conversely, EMAs may briefly cross during normal pullbacks without invalidating the trend.

As a result:
- Positions can remain open during clear structure failure,
- Or risk logic becomes implicitly dependent on EMA behavior rather than price action.

EMA crosses should be treated as **informational context**, not as direct exit triggers.

---

## Desired Outcome

- Decouple trade exits from raw EMA cross events.
- Introduce **explicit structure‑fail exit conditions** based on price behavior.
- Preserve the existing rule that EMA cross blocks **new scale‑ins**, but does not force exits.
- Make exit behavior deterministic, explainable, and testable.

---

## Requirements

### 1. EMA Cross Reclassification
- EMA(8/21) cross against the position:
  - **Must NOT** trigger an exit.
  - **Must** continue to block new scale‑ins (existing behavior preserved).
- EMA cross is treated as **context only**, not execution logic.

### 2. Structure-Fail Exit Conditions
Define explicit structure-failure signals that may trigger an exit (configurable):

Examples (choose one or more, defaults defined below):
- Close beyond EMA(21) by > X ticks.
- Break of last impulse low (long) or high (short).
- Consecutive closes against trend with declining momentum.
- Price acceptance back inside a defined range after breakout.

Default behavior:
- Structure-fail exits flatten **remaining position** (market or stop logic as appropriate).

### 3. Integration with Existing Risk Systems
- Structure-fail logic runs **after**:
  - Decoupled stop logic (TICKET‑027),
  - Tiered pyramiding (TICKET‑028),
  - Heat budget enforcement (TICKET‑029),
  - Give‑back governor (TICKET‑030).
- Structure-fail exit must never:
  - Increase risk,
  - Re-enter automatically,
  - Override a tighter stop already in place.

### 4. Configuration Parameters (with defaults)
- `EnableStructureFailExit = true`
- `StructureFailMode = EMA21Break | ImpulseBreak`
- `EMA21BreakTicks = X`
- `MinBarsInTradeBeforeStructureCheck = N`

### 5. Logging & Diagnostics
Log when structure-fail logic triggers:
- Active structure-fail condition
- Price and EMA values
- Position size at exit
- Whether exit was partial or full
