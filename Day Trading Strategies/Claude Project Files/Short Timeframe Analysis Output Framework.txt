# Enhanced Technical Analysis Framework
Version 1.0

## 1. Initial Context (25-50 words)
- Ticker symbol & timeframe analyzed
- Current market session & conditions
- Key market indices status
- Notable sector activity
- Recent volatility context (ATR)

## 2. TLDR Summary (Single focused paragraph, max 75 words)
- Clear directional bias (LONG/SHORT/NEUTRAL)
One or two sentence trade recommendation with direction and key levels.

## 3. Candlestick Analysis 
### Recent Price Action
- Individual candle analysis (last 3-5 candles) 
  - Doji patterns
  - Hammer/Inverted hammer signals
  - Long body candles
  - Topping/Bottoming tails
- Multi-candle patterns
  - Trend direction
  - Momentum assessment
  - Volume correlation

### Pattern Recognition
Focus on TCG's primary patterns:
1. Double Bottom/Top
   - Support/resistance levels
   - Volume confirmation
   - Stop placement zones
2. Equilibrium/Symmetrical Triangle
   - Trend line convergence
   - Volume contraction
   - Breakout targets
3. Head & Shoulders
   - Neckline identification
   - Volume profile
   - Target measurement

## 4. Moving Average Analysis
### Core EMAs (9, 20, 50, 200)
- Price relationship to each EMA
- EMA stacking order
- Crossover signals

### TCG EMA Strategies
1. Smush Zone Setup
   - EMA compression identification
   - Volume characteristics
   - Breakout potential

2. Double Tap Pattern
   - EMA resistance/support levels
   - Price rejection points
   - Trading range definition

3. Flipperoo Signals
   - Dominant EMA identification
   - Support/resistance flip points
   - Follow-through confirmation

## 5. Technical Indicator Suite
### Primary Indicators
- RSI (Relative Strength Index)
  - Overbought/oversold levels
  - Divergence patterns
  - Trend strength

- Bollinger Bands
  - Price location within bands
  - Band width trends
  - Extreme moves (outside bands)

### Volume Analysis
- Current vs. Average volume
- Volume trend analysis
- Price/volume relationships
- Key volume nodes

## 6. Risk Management Framework
### Position Sizing
- Account risk parameters (1-2% max per trade)
- ATR-based position sizing
- Leverage considerations
- Scaling rules

### Stop Loss Strategy
- Technical stop placement
- ATR-based stop ranges
- Time-based stops
- Scale-out levels

### Profit Targets
- Primary target (2:1 minimum R:R)
- Secondary targets
- Trailing stop methodology
- Scale-out points

## 7. Trade Execution Plan
### Entry Strategy
Base Position (40%)
- Entry price zones
- Stop placement
- Initial target
- Dollar risk amount

Scaling Plan (if confirmed)
- Second entry (30%) conditions
- Final entry (30%) conditions
- Stop adjustments
- Target adjustments

### Trade Management
- Scale-out levels
- Stop adjustment rules
- Time-based management
- Pattern invalidation points

## 8. Market Context
- Market breadth indicators
- Sector rotation analysis
- Correlated assets
- Key economic events

## 9. Psychological Conditions
- Fear/Greed assessment
- Momentum characteristics
- Crowd psychology indicators
- FOMO potential

## 10. Trade Journal
Entry Analysis:
- Setup quality (1-10)
- Pattern clarity
- Risk/reward ratio
- Technical alignment

Exit Analysis:
- Target achievement
- Stop execution
- Scale-out effectiveness
- Strategy adherence

Lessons Learned:
- What worked
- What didn't work
- Adjustments needed
- Psychology notes

## Pre-Analysis Checklist
1. Data Verification
   - Price data current
   - Volume data valid
   - Indicator calculations verified
   - Timeframe alignment checked

2. Pattern Validation
   - Multiple timeframe confirmation
   - Volume confirmation
   - Key level identification
   - Risk/reward minimum 2:1

3. Risk Assessment
   - Position size calculated
   - Stop loss placement valid
   - Maximum risk exposure defined
   - Pattern invalidation clear

## Update Format (Intraday)
Brief update on:
1. Pattern development
2. Technical changes
3. Volume conditions
4. New opportunities
5. Risk adjustments

Note: All analysis should prioritize risk management and align with TCG's principles of disciplined trading and emotional control.
